We are right here in your world.

We do what we do very well, or not at all. We aim to remove complexity; we do not look to make routine legal issues complex. We leave this to others.

View the team

Barlow Robbins is right here, in your world, helping you find your way through a wide range of issues for which you might require sound legal advice.

We place you and your outcome at the centre of everything we do. Our aim? That you will feel strongly supported, informed and in control with advice that’s prompt, incisive and clearly communicated.

We focus on outcomes

We can help you to grow your business, to safeguard your personal wealth, to protect the reputation of your school or charity and act for those who have suffered serious personal injury.

  • Individuals
  • Businesses

  • Family

    We live in a changing and complex world. Families are more complex and diverse than they used to be. We can advise you on all aspects of your family's affairs; from the start of your adult life when you are planning your future to protecting your assets and family interests as you grow older.

    • Children & Family Matters
    • Divorce & Separation
    • Asset & Wealth Protection
    • Residential Property

  • Property

    For most of us our home is the largest asset we own. We can help you with all aspects of buying, selling and letting property, including residential, agricultural/equestrian and buy to let properties.

    • Residential Property
    • Agricultural and Equestrian Property
    • Wealth & Asset Protection
    • Property Litigation

  • The Unexpected

    Your life can be turned upside down by family break up, unexpected illness or serious injury, career setbacks and legal disputes. We can help guide you through these varied, challenging and complex experiences to get your life back on track.

    • Separation & Divorce
    • Personal Injury or Medical Malpractice
    • Employment Issues
    • Personal Disputes

  • Other Matters

    Legal problems can arise anywhere and everywhere. Elderly relatives may need assistance getting their affairs in order if they have become infirm or incapacitated, you may wish to plan for your children or grandchildren or you may need the services of a notary.

    • Advice for Elderly People
    • Trusts
    • Notary Public

  • Starting Up

    Starting a business can be both challenging and rewarding, whether you are a first time entrepreneur or an overseas business launching in the UK market.

    • Commercial
    • Company Secretarial
    • Corporate Tax
    • Intellectual Property

  • Growing

    If your business is to grow successfully, it needs to be legally sound. We can help you along the journey, looking after a range of legal issues crucial to your success.

    • Banking & Finance
    • Commercial
    • Commercial Property
    • Employment Issues
    • Intellectual Property, Technology & Media

  • Protecting

    As your business becomes more complex, your advisory needs grow too. These issues can be wide ranging from day to day matters affecting owners, directors, shareholders and employees to securing commercial property or even dealing with business disputes.

    • Business Agreements
    • Business Disputes
    • Commercial Property
    • Employment Issues
    • Corporate

  • Exiting

    Eventually you may want to realise the value of your business. Exit strategies can include family succession, sale to management, trade or private equity buyers, flotation on a public exchange or winding up.

    • Mergers & Acquisitions
    • Corporate Tax
    • Wealth & Asset Protection
